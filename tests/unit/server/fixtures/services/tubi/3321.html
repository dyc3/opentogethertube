Kitchen Nightmares

(2014)
TV-14
Reality
World-famous chef Gordon Ramsay gives struggling restaurants across the United States a week of his time, advice, and a fighting chance to succeed.

Jay Hunter
Brad Kreisberg
Kent Weed

STARRING

J.V. Martin
